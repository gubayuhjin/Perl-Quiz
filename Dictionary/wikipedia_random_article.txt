<p>The <b>Winona County Courthouse</b> in the city of <a href="/wiki/Winona,_Minnesota" title="Winona, Minnesota">Winona</a>, <a href="/wiki/Winona_County,_Minnesota">Winona County, Minnesota</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">U.S.</a> is a <a href="/wiki/Richardsonian_Romanesque">Richardsonian Romanesque</a> structure built in 1888. It was the first courthouse in Minnesota listed on the <a href="/wiki/National_Register_of_Historic_Places">National Register of Historic Places</a>, having been added in 1970.<sup id="cite_ref-byways_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-byways-1"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> The original cost of the courthouse was $125,000.<sup id="cite_ref-county_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-county-2"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>The building was renovated in 1971 after its listing on the National Register of Historic Places. Up until 2000, nearly $2.5 million was spent restoring and remodeling the facility. On September 3, 2000, fourth-floor fire suppression sprinkler piping burst, flooding the building with over 4500 gallons of water and collapsing ceilings and more. The county offices were relocated to other buildings, and the building was again renovated to repair water damage and to bring the building up to current standards.<sup id="cite_ref-county_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-county-2"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> Insurance paid for over half the cost of repairs, and the <a href="/wiki/Minnesota_Historical_Society">Minnesota Historical Society</a> contributed $50,000 for exterior work. The renovation also returned much of the building to its original appearance, including even the old fireplaces.<sup id="cite_ref-byways_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-byways-1"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> The total cost of interior renovation was $5.6 million, with another $1.5 million spent on external renovation.<sup id="cite_ref-mncourts_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mncourts-3"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
