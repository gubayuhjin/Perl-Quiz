[edit] German

[edit] Etymology

Old High German list, from Germanic *listiz. Cognate with obsolete English list.

[edit] Pronunciation

  • IPA: /lɪst/

[edit] Noun

List f. (genitive List, plural Listen)

  1. cunning; craft
  2. trick

[edit] Usage notes

  • "mit List und Tücke" may be translated as "with patience and a snare"
