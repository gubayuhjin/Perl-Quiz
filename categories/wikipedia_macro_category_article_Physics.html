Physics (from the Greek φυσικός (phisikos), "natural", and φύσις (phisis), "nature") is the science of the natural world in the broadest sense of that term. It deals with matter and energy, and the fundamental forces of nature that govern the interactions between particles. It was known as natural philosophy until the late 19th century.

The main article for this category is Physics.
